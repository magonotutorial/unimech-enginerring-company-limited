Company overview

UNIMECH ENGINEERING COMPANY LIMITED was founded in 2023 with a vision to create history in the mechanical engineering field. With three dedicated directors and a proficient secretary, the company has quickly become a force to reckon with in providing professional, scientific, and technical activities. Our services extend to the repair of fabricated metal products, personal service activities, steam and air conditioning supply, support activities for mining and quarrying, materials , mechanical design, maintenance, and industrial production activities.
